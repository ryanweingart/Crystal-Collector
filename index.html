Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your score goes above the random number, you lose. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. The value of the crystals will change with each new game.

Good luck!
